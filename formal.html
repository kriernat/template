Executive Summary

Literacy is something that everyone encounters every day without even realizing it. Literacy is the possession of knowledge whether it is through reading, writing or research. Literacy plays a big role in any career path but in packaging, it includes several different aspects. One of the reasons why packaging is such an interesting career path is because it involves so many different subjects. Packaging is a major many people may not know about but it involves the designing of packaging for products while considering many different subjects such as math, science, business, reading, writing, and communication. The diverse skillset a packaging professional must possess makes a packaging professional and well-rounded individual. A packaging professional must be literate in all of these subjects in order to have a successful career as a packaging professional.
